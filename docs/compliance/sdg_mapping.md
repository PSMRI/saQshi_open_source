## Purpose

This document maps SaQshi to Sustainable Development Goal relevance for Digital Public Good readiness review.

## Primary SDG Alignment

| SDG | Alignment |
| --- | --- |
| SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being | SaQshi supports healthcare quality assessment, continuous quality improvement, certification tracking, performance monitoring and public health facility monitoring. |

## Supporting SDG Alignment

| SDG | Alignment |
| --- | --- |
| SDG 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure | The platform provides reusable digital public infrastructure patterns for healthcare quality monitoring. |
| SDG 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions | Role-based monitoring, audit-ready workflows, documented governance and transparent reporting support institutional accountability. |
| SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals | Open documentation, APIs, JSON-driven configuration and GPL-3.0 licensing support reuse by public-sector and partner organizations. |

## Public Benefit

SaQshi is intended to help health systems:

- Measure facility quality consistently.
- Convert assessment gaps into action plans.
- Track improvement over time.
- Monitor facility performance and certification status.
- Support district, division, state and programme review using structured data.
